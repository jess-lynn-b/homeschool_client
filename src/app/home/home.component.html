Homeschool Life


Welcome to a new way of schooling. Homeschool life is more than books and tests. It bring everyday learning and incorporates it into life skills to help your child be succussful both academically and in life.

Homeschool can either be religous basesd or not based on what fits your family values. Homeschool can be all on the computer or all work books as well as a combination of both. This is almost complete freedom when it comes to your childs education. Every child is different in what they need to be successful and this allows you to tailor thier education to what they need. Homeschool life will not look the same for everyone as in the set up or how everyday fuctions and that is ok.

Make sure to check out the requirements for the sate you reside in when it comes to the Homeschool laws and regualtions. In the requirements tab is the ones for Missouri as well as some resourses. If you have any questions or suggestions make sure to click on 'Contact Us' above. Congratualtions on making this journey into the Homeschool world it is what you make of it!
